Description

The International Humanity Foundation (IHF) is proud to present its esteemed Internship Program, offering a remarkable opportunity to gain valuable GLOBAL experience for your resume. For over 20 years, IHF has been at the forefront, providing hands-on Global Internships and Volunteer experiences across a diverse range of fields. Now, you have the chance to enrich your resume with international exposure, all from the comfort of your home.

We currently seek online volunteers and interns from around the globe to join our dynamic teams in four active divisions. As part of these divisions, you will not only contribute to meaningful projects but also acquire essential life skills that will set you apart in your professional journey. The four divisions seeking enthusiastic participants are the Human Resources Division, Finance & Fundraising Division, Online Mentors & Education Division, and Media PR Division.

By becoming a part of the IHF Internship Program, you will be empowered to make a difference globally and engage in transformative experiences that will shape your future. Embrace the opportunity to complete your degree Internship or Volunteer hours while immersing yourself in a diverse and inclusive environment.

To take the first step towards a rewarding global experience, visit our website at www.ihfonline.org, where you can learn more about the program and begin your application process. Don't miss out on this chance to make a meaningful impact and foster your personal and professional growth with the International Humanity Foundation.
